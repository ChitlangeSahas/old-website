ELECTRIC UNICYCLE


Uniwheel


Myself and a Computer Science colleague David wanted to experiment with building a lightweight self-balancing electric vehicle, similar to hoverboards and segways.
We applied the inverted pendulum balancing techniques to keep the wheel under the user's center of gravity. Uniwheel uses leaning to drive forward and back, as well as turning.
